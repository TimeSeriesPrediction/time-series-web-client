The NewGen Leaders

Meet The Team

A group of hard-working individuals, dedicated to turning large amounts of coffee into highly efficient and working code.



As a team, we pride ourselves in all the work we do, development and otherwise. We try to ensure we produce only the most efficient and robust code possible, while attempting to maintain a level of sanity lost to many a developer. By keeping work serious, yet interaction light hearted, we can achieve a balance between efficiency and approachability that ensures team members can find a way to pull through those long coffee filled nights, as well as interact with clients in a way that isn't as robotic as a conversation with Siri. It is a given with a profession in any technological industry, that keeping up to date with the latest trends ensures the most efficient design. We believe that by making use of only the newest stable frameworks available, we ensure a higher level of security, compatibility, and not to mention internet resources available. Due to this, we as a team are always looking for a challenge, as well as a chance to tackle new opportunities and technologies.
